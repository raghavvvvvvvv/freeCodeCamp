Mr. Bean

The man who made a billion people laugh

Here's a time line of Mr. Bean's life:

  • 1955: Birth of Rowan Atkinson, the actor behind Mr. Bean, on January 6th.
  • 1989: The first episode of "Mr. Bean" television series is aired, introducing the world to the iconic character.
  • 1990: "Mr. Bean" wins the British Academy Television Award for Best Entertainment Performance.
  • 1997: The "Bean" movie is released, bringing Mr. Bean's misadventures to the big screen and gaining international acclaim.
  • 2002: The animated series "Mr. Bean: The Animated Series" is launched, continuing the character's comedic legacy.
  • 2007: Mr. Bean appears in the opening ceremony of the London 2012 Olympic Games, delighting the audience with his unique style.
  • 2019: Rowan Atkinson announces that he is retiring the character of Mr. Bean, marking the end of an era in comedy.

"People think because I can make them laugh on the stage, I'll be able to make them laugh in person. That isn't the case at all. I am essentially a rather quiet, dull person who just happens to be a performer."

-- Rowan Atkinson aka Mr. Bean
